## Component Hierarchy

```
PlayerCardShowcase
├── EnhancedPlayerRankingCard (main)
│   ├── Front (player info)
│   │   ├── Rarity badge
│   │   ├── Rank indicator
│   │   ├── Player number
│   │   ├── Level progress
│   │   └── Stats grid
│   └── Back (attributes)
│       └── 6 attribute bars
├── PlayerStatRadar (optional)
│   ├── Background grid
│   ├── Data polygon
│   ├── Vertex points
│   └── Labels
└── PlayerCardComparison (modal)
    ├── Card grid (2-4 players)
    ├── Overall comparison
    ├── Attribute breakdown
    └── Progress bars
```

## State Flow

```
[User Opens Page]
       ↓
[Load Player Data]
       ↓
[Render Card (Front)]
       ↓
  ┌────┴────┐
  ↓         ↓
[Hover]  [Click Flip]
  ↓         ↓
[Shimmer] [Show Back]
  ↓         ↓
[Normal]  [Click Flip]
           ↓
      [Show Front]
           ↓
    [Click Export]
           ↓
    [Generate PNG]
           ↓
    [Download/Share]
```
